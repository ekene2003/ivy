Our Story

From a Simple Dream to Nigeria's Trusted Wellness Brand

IvyDealz was founded in 2022 right here in Lagos by a passionate team of wellness enthusiasts who were frustrated by one thing: incredible health and beauty products were either too expensive, hard to find, or simply not available in Nigeria.

We started small sourcing carefully selected, NAFDAC-approved herbal remedies and personal care gadgets and delivering them directly to Nigerians' doors. Word spread fast. Our customers saw real results, and they told their friends, their sisters, their mothers.

Today, IvyDealz serves over 1,700 satisfied customers across all 36 states. Our product range has grown from infection cures to a full wellness and skincare collection but our mission remains the same: bring you premium products that actually work, at prices that are fair.
